About Me

Nothing about science but more about personality...

I used to care much about what others think of me, and how I am able to convince people that I can be as good as others. Now I only care about how much 'I', at this moment, can be better than the I, say, one day before.

I used to believe who I am is what exactly people judged. Now I only care about who I want to be and how much I am able to be dedicated.

I used to believe there were no more opportunities, not even a single chance for me, for many times. However, I survived through.

The answer turned out to be - I can never be anyone else, nor as good as others. I can only be I.

即使只能仰望，也要挺胸看前路，收起玻璃心。

坚持做一件事情，即使看起来再愚蠢，也没有解释的必要。

I deeply appreciate that one can achieve a challenging goal if one approaches it seriously.

Most successful Chinese students study very hard to get high scores on exams, but my personality is not a good match for this system since I am motivated to work on the things that interest me. Because I did not do particularly well on the college entrance exams, I was not admitted to the top universities. Consequently, I never even saw a real research laboratory while I was in college.

然而我深切的认为，我非常的幸运。无论身在何处，都能知遇恩师的帮助和提点。他们所有人都在自己力所能及的范围内给我提供最好的条件，让我有机会在自身起点很低的情况下学的尽可能的快。信任给我最大的动力。

I have been so lucky to have great supervisors who believed in me, even though I was not an elite college student. My first supervisor, taught me how to think like a scientist and was patient enough to teach me basic experimental skills. I will always appreciate his tolerance and patience, and try to treat my own students in a similar way. After I received my Master’s degree, I realized that I should try to get more experience and systematic training in a good lab. However, I applied to the lab of Dr. Guohong Li lab and to my great fortune, he took a chance and admitted me into his lab as a PhD student, although he had many candidates who were more qualified “on paper.” In his lab I learned so much, and I was also inspired by his diligence and perseverance.

I am just ordinary. What I can do is to make the most of the opportunities I have had.

Now, I feel so lucky again, that I am working with great mentors, and a bunch of extremely awesome colleagues here in the lab and in Berkeley. This is the environment that fosters me, allows me to grow, further.

所以我能做的就是，缓慢的旅途中学会享受沿途的风景，跛脚前行方能踏出不寻常的足迹。
